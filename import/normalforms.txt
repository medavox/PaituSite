======Normal Forms======
Different ...standards? ideals? aspirations? for databases. Dry theory.
=====Normal Form (1NF)=====

  * No composite attributes
    * Can't have 'Name' field with sub-fields 'First Name' and 'Last Name'
  * only one value per attribute
    * eg, can't have list of marks in a single 'marks' field
  * No repeating fields

=====Second Normal Form (2NF)=====

Must be 1NF, plus:
  *Every non-prime attribute is dependent on the primary key
